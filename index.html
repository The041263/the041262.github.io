My name is Tracy H, and I’m currently a first-year student at the University of California, Riverside, majoring in Media and Cultural Studies. I’m passionate about understanding how media shapes our perceptions, influences culture, and reflects social values. Whether it’s film, digital content, or social media platforms, I’m fascinated by the ways stories are told and received in today’s global landscape.

Through my coursework, I’ve started exploring topics like media theory, representation, and cultural identity. These subjects have helped me think more critically about the content we consume and how it impacts real-world conversations around race, gender, and power. I’m particularly interested in how digital media is changing traditional narratives and offering new platforms for marginalized voices.

Outside the classroom, I enjoy writing, editing videos, and analyzing popular media trends. I hope to gain experience in media production and eventually work in content creation, journalism, or media strategy. As I continue my studies, I look forward to growing both academically and creatively while contributing to meaningful conversations in and beyond the classroom.
